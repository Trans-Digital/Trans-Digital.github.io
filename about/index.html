About


Trans_ is the first anthology to collect the voices and experiences of trans people speaking to how the Internet has impacted our lives and how we have impacted the Internet. Publication will be Summber 2016 est.


Editors: Mitch Kellaway (@MitchKellaway), Harlan Kellaway (@HarlanKellaway), TBD
